# Simple Recipes

## 1. Fluffy Pancakes
- Prep: 5 min · Cook: 10 min · Serves: 2–3

### Ingredients
- 1 cup all-purpose flour  
- 2 tbsp sugar  
- 1 tbsp baking powder  
- 1/4 tsp salt  
- 1 cup milk  
- 1 large egg  
- 2 tbsp melted butter (plus extra for the pan)  
- 1 tsp vanilla (optional)

### Steps
1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.  
2. In another bowl, beat milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.  
3. Combine wet into dry; stir until just mixed (small lumps OK).  
4. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium, melt a little butter.  
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake; cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook 1–2 min.  
6. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or yogurt.

### Tips
- Don't overmix. Rest batter 5–10 min for extra fluffiness.

## 2. Quick Tomato-Basil Pasta
- Prep: 5 min · Cook: 15 min · Serves: 2

### Ingredients
- 200 g pasta (spaghetti or penne)  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 cloves garlic, sliced  
- 400 g cherry tomatoes (halved) or canned diced tomatoes  
- Salt and pepper to taste  
- Handful fresh basil leaves, torn  
- Grated Parmesan (optional)

### Steps
1. Cook pasta until al dente; reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.  
2. Sauté garlic in olive oil until fragrant. Add tomatoes, season, and simmer 5–8 min.  
3. Toss pasta with sauce, add reserved water to loosen if needed.  
4. Stir in basil and top with Parmesan.

### Variations
- Add chili flakes for heat or a splash of balsamic for sweetness.

## 3. Lemon-Garlic Salmon (Sheet Pan)
- Prep: 5 min · Cook: 12–15 min · Serves: 2

### Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets (~150–200 g each)  
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 lemon (zest + slices)  
- Salt and pepper  
- Optional: chopped parsley

### Steps
1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Line a baking sheet.  
2. Mix oil, garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Brush over salmon. Top with lemon slices.  
3. Bake 12–15 min until salmon flakes easily. Garnish with parsley.

### Tip
- For crispy edges, broil 1–2 min at the end, watching closely.
